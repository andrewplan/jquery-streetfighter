To get Ryu ready to fight, hover your mouse cursor over him.

To have Ryu fire a Hadouken, click on him.

To have Ryu strike a pose, press and hold down the "x" key.

To get Hulk Ryu ready to fight, hover your mouse cursor over him.

To have Hulk Ryu fire a Hadouken, click on him.

To have Hulk Ryu strike a pose, press and hold down the "Enter" key.
